We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password (encrypted) for authentication purposes.

  • Device Information: We may collect device identifiers for analytics and advertising purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App
  • Create and manage your account
  • Track your quiz progress and display results
  • Send you notifications related to your account or quiz activity
  • Display personalized advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

3. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy

4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our App. AdMob may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information for advertising purposes. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings or visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your password is encrypted and stored securely using Firebase Authentication. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is designed for general audiences and is not specifically targeted at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your account and data
